Academic Programs
Programs We Offer
Undergraduate and postgraduate programs affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), designed to meet industry standards.
💻

B.Tech — Computer Science

Core programming, data structures, algorithms, AI/ML, cloud computing and software engineering for modern tech roles.

4 Years · 240 Credits
⚙️

B.Tech — Mechanical Engineering

Thermodynamics, manufacturing processes, CAD/CAM, and design engineering with hands-on workshop training.

4 Years · 240 Credits
⚡

B.Tech — Electrical Engineering

Power systems, control engineering, electronics, and renewable energy technologies with practical lab exposure.

4 Years · 240 Credits

B.Tech — Civil Engineering

Structural analysis, construction management, surveying, and sustainable infrastructure design.

4 Years · 240 Credits

B.Tech — Electronics & Comm.

Signal processing, VLSI design, communication systems, and embedded technologies for core ECE roles.

4 Years · 240 Credits

MBA — Management Studies

Business strategy, marketing, finance, HR and entrepreneurship for future business leaders.

2 Years · 120 Credits
